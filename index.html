// Who I Am

Not a Conference
Room Guy.

I didn't start in a conference room. I started on the floor, learning what actually matters in an operation with my hands. That's shaped every decision I've made as a leader since.

Most plants don't fail because of bad equipment. They fail because accountability gets soft, standards start to slip, and nobody wants to say anything about it. I've seen it happen. My job is to make sure it doesn't.

I lead people the way I'd want to be led. Honest. Clear expectations. Genuine investment in their growth. When people know where they stand and believe someone's actually in their corner, they show up differently. And when people show up differently, the plant runs differently.

Productivity is not accidental. Reliability is not luck. Performance becomes repeatable when ownership is non-negotiable.

I'm not chasing the next title. I'm chasing better operations and better leaders. That's the work.

Operational Excellence

Lean principles, preventive maintenance discipline, and KPI driven decision making that reduce unplanned downtime and sustain throughput under pressure.

People Leadership

Building teams where ownership replaces blame, clarity replaces confusion, and individuals grow into stronger leaders than when they arrived.

Future Focused Execution

Evaluating digital tools and analytics not by what they promise, but by whether the foundation is ready to support them. Technology doesn't replace experience. It preserves it.
