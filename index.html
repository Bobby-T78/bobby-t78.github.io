About

Standards win the long game.

Most aggregate operations don't fail because of bad equipment or bad people. They fail because standards erode, ownership blurs, and accountability becomes optional.

My focus is the opposite: building operations where expectations are clear, ownership is shared, and performance is repeatable. I lead by aligning people, process, and technology so that results hold up long after the spotlight moves on.

The goal is simple — leave operations stronger than I found them, and develop people capable of carrying them even further.

How I Lead
